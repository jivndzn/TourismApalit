Welcome to Apalit

Welcome to the Municipality of Apalit

Nestled in the heart of Central Luzon, the picturesque municipality of Apalit, located in the province of Pampanga, Philippines, beckons travelers and explorers with its multifaceted tapestry of history, culture, and natural allure.

Location

Apalit is positioned in the southern part of Pampanga bounded in the south by Calumpit, Bulacan, in the west by the metropolis of Macabebe, and San Simon in the north. Apalit was named after a big sturdy tree with the scientific name “Pterocarpus indicus” also known as “Narra,” known by Kapampangan as “Apalit.”
